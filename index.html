About Marcus

M. Aurelius Higgs is the founder of ParentWise and creator of The Story Growth Method, a science-backed approach that helps multicultural, entrepreneurial parents move from creating a safe home to helping their teen build safety within themselves.

With over 20 years as an international educator across 10+ countries, Marcus translates developmental psychology into practical tools that build lasting family security from the inside out.

Signature Talks
01
Create Security During Adolescence
How to Build Your Teen's Confidence to Face an Uncertain Future
  • Discover why your teen's security is built on yours, and what that means for how you show up daily
  • Identify the hidden patterns from your own upbringing that are quietly shaping your teen's emotional world
  • Learn the difference between rescuing a struggling teen and supporting them, and why that difference builds or breaks confidence
02
Expand Your 3 Circles of Trust
Become Your Family's Foundation for a Secure Future
  • Map the 3 Circles of Trust that every secure parent needs and where most teens are lacking
  • Build weekly rituals that create real, consistent well-being before crisis demands it

The Motivation Switch
